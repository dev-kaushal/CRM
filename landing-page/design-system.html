2. Typography

Display Font: Cause

The quick brown fox

Used exclusively for Section Titles, Logos, and Hero Statements.

Body Font: Inter

Inter is a typeface carefully crafted & designed for computer screens. It features a tall x-height to aid in readability of mixed-case and lower-case text. Used for all paragraphs, buttons, and UI elements.
